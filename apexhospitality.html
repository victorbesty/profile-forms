This questionnaire is organized into 15 categories covering the full operational surface area — from day-to-day guest issues through governance and reporting. Answer what you can in the moment; come back to anything that needs a stakeholder check.

Brief answers are fine. Where a question doesn't apply, write "N/A." When you're done, use the buttons at the bottom to download your responses or print to PDF.
