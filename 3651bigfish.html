How to fill this out

This form captures the operational decisions that drive every guest interaction — from first inquiry to post-stay. Some sections are short; others (playbooks, FAQs) are deeper. You don't have to do it in one sitting.

Where we have a sensible default, we've noted it. Where there's a recommendation based on what works for premium oceanfront, we say so. Everything's editable.

One policy per questionNot one per agent's preference. We're documenting how the property is run, not how individuals work.
Property-specific exceptionsIf a rule changes for one unit, call it out in the notes box at the end of each section.
"TBD" is fineIf you don't know yet, write "TBD" — we'd rather flag the gap than fill it with guesses.
